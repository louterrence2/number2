My Analysis Dashboards

A collection of custom-built tools for market analysis.

The Global Macro Dashboard

Analyzes major commodities against their long-term, inflation-adjusted price history to find cyclical opportunities in energy, agriculture, and industrial sectors.

E&P Oil Producers Value Tracker

A definitive analysis of oil and gas producers, featuring valuation ranges, debt metrics, and AI-powered outlooks for each company.

Insurance & Financial Services Tracker

A comparative analysis of major insurance companies, with key valuation metrics and a qualitative AI rating on their business model and outlook.

Crypto Miner Leverage Tracker

Tracks the leverage of publicly traded Bitcoin miners against their crypto holdings, providing insight into their risk profile in a volatile market.
